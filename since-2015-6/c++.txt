perf
    L1 is the Level-1 cache, the smallest and fastest one.
    LLC on the other hand refers to the last level cache
    i vs. d distinguishes instruction cache from data cache.
        Only L1 is split in this way, other caches are shared between data and instructions.
    TLB refers to the translation lookaside buffer,
        a cache used when mapping virtual addresses to physical ones.
